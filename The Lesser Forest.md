#region

The Lesser Forest is a Subtropical Rainforest bordering [[./The Verdant Groves|The Verdant Groves]], [[./The Spellwrought Jungles|The Spellwrought Jungles]], [[./The Southern Mountains|The Southern Mountains]], and [[./The Iron Swamps|The Iron Swamps]]. It's only settlement is the city state of [[./Galemont|Galemont]], nestled at the base of [[./Deepsuth Mont|Deepsuth Mont]].
