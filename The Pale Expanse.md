#region

The Pale Expanse is a large desert taking up most of the Southwestern section of the continent, bordered by [[./The Southern Mountains|The Southern Mountains]] (and [[./Deepsuth Mont|Deepsuth Mont]]), and [[./The Western Drought|The Western Drought]] to the North. It rests along the coast of the Western Ocean. It's culture is dominated by the Arab-inspired Azure Sultanate.
