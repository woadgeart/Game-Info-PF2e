#region
The Middle Crossing is an economically significant zone on the continent, being the widest opening in the central mountains that marks the separation of the [[./Northern Mountain Range|Northern Mountain Range]] and [[./The Southern Mountains|The Southern Mountains]]. 

It is home to the twin city-states, [[./Slipbrook|Slipbrook]] and [[./Westerwich|Westerwich]], as well as the township of [[./Dark Pass|Dark Pass]] which sits between them. Almost all trade between the East and West passes through the Middle Crossing.

It is bordered by [[./The Verdant Groves|The Verdant Groves]] to the East, and [[./The Western Drought|The Western Drought]] to the West.
