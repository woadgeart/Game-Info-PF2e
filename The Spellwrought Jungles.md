#region

The Spellwrought Jungles are a magically volatile region on the continent, being where most [[Ley Lines|Ley Lines]] converge as a result of it sitting on a tectonic intersection. It is a tropical rainforest with only one city state, and one port town; [[./Spellridge|Spellridge]] and [[./Warmglade|Warmglade]], respectively. It is bordered by the [[./Snowland|Snowland]] strait to the South, and [[./The Iron Swamps|The Iron Swamps]] to the Northwest, though it is separated from most of the rest of the continent by massive mountains known as the [[./Spellridge Walls|Spellridge Walls]]; there is a gap in the mountains known as the [[./Spellstrait|Spellstrait]].
