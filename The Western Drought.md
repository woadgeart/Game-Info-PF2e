#region

The Western Drought is a massive dry region stretching from [[./The Pale Expanse|The Pale Expanse]] to the south, [[./Iceland|Iceland]] in the North, the Western Ocean, and the [[./Northern Mountain Range|Northern Mountain Range]] and [[./The Middle Crossing|The Middle Crossing]] to the East. It ranges from Tundra in the far north to savannah in the center, and desert in the south.
