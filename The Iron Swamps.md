#region

The Iron Swamps is a marshy region in the Southern half of the continent, stretching from [[./The Lesser Forest|The Lesser Forest]] to the [[./Snowland|Snowland]] strait. It is home to the city-state of [[./Ironmill|Ironmill]], and it's three satellite port towns, [[./Rosenspell|Rosenspell]], [[./Decastop|Decastop]], and [[./Mortarleigh|Mortarleigh]].
