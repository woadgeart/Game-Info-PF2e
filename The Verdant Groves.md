#region
The Verdant Groves is a massive temperate forest that stretches from [[./The Great Forest|The Great Forest]] in the North, [[./The Lesser Forest|The Lesser Forest]] in the South, the Eastern Ocean to the East, and [[./The Middle Crossing|The Middle Crossing]], [[./Northern Mountain Range|Northern Mountain Range]], and [[./The Southern Mountains|The Southern Mountains]] to the West. It is the second largest region on the continent by area, with the first being the Western Drought. 

It is home to two city states; [[./Amberquay|Amberquay]] and [[./Mystpines|Mystpines]].
