*Rules of the Universe*
1. Cosmology
	1. There are parallel universes in the standard pathfinder cosmology
	2. These realms are connected only at portals and other magical rifts, and largely overlap; elements are quantum-entangled with themselves on their respective plane. This means all living beings have a quantum shadow in all transitive planes, as well as the positive and negative energy planes; while entering a transitive plane means permanently bonding with your quantum shadow (thus granting abnormal and deviant abilities and mutations and forever erasing a version of yourself), entering a positive or negative energy plane without adequate magical protection violently collides the person's matter with their anti-matter or super-matter and violently destroys all versions of the person.
2. The great beyond, aka Space, is the home of almost all of the gods; in the great beyond, the rifts between realities are easier to open, and the barriers between the planes are thinner in a vacuum. Thus, it is far easier to access other planes from space, making space travel a theoretically viable method for transcending the mortal plane.
